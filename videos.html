How to Add Videos

  1. Go to your YouTube channel
  2. Click on any video you want to embed
  3. Copy the video ID from the URL (the part after ?v=)
  4. Add the video ID to the videoIds array in the JavaScript section below
  5. The video will automatically appear on this page

Example: If your video URL is youtube.com/watch?v=abc123xyz, then add 'abc123xyz' to the videoIds array.
