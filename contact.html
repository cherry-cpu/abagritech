Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase your products?

You can contact us directly through phone or email, or reach out to our field officers in your area. We also work with authorized distributors across various regions.

Are your products certified organic?

Yes, all our products are 100% organic and certified. They are safe for crops, farmers, and the environment with no harmful chemical residues.

Do you provide technical support?

Absolutely! Our team of field officers and agricultural experts are available to provide guidance on product usage, best practices, and crop management.

Can I become a distributor?

We welcome partnership opportunities. Please contact us through the form above or email us at info@abagritech.com with your business details.

What is the shelf life of your products?

Our products typically have a shelf life of 2-3 years when stored properly in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Specific details are mentioned on each product label.
